Score entry is not a task.

Most teachers who feel overwhelmed during their administration window have one thing in common: they saved all their score entry for the end. This section is about a better approach.

The Reality

Fitting it all into a busy school day

The KRA includes many items. Teachers often begin administering it while still establishing classroom routines and getting to know their students.

In practice, the teachers who feel most on top of their window are not the ones who work harder. They are the ones who build entry into their week, a little at a time, rather than saving it for the end.

The Key Idea

Check the Item To Do List at the end of every week. It shows you exactly what still needs attention before you lose track of it.

One report. Five minutes on Friday. That is the habit that keeps teachers ahead of their window instead of scrambling at the end of it.
